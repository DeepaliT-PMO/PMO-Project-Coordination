## â— Problem Statement
Professionals and small businesses often rely on traditional paper business cards or fragmented digital tools, making contact sharing, profile updates, and follow-ups inefficient.

Existing solutions lack a centralized, scalable platform that allows users to create, update, and share digital business profiles seamlessly across devices while providing visibility into usage and engagement.

From a business perspective, this leads to:
- Poor user experience during networking
- Difficulty maintaining updated contact information
- Limited engagement and usage visibility
- Inefficient onboarding and profile management
